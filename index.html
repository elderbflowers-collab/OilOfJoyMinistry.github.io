You are not alone.

Genesis 28:16, NLT

"Surely the Lord is in this place, and I wasn't even aware of it!"

Jacob was running, tired, and sleeping on a stone — but God was already there.

Grace doesn't wait for us to get it together; God meets us in the middle of our mess.

God is with you now.

— Pastor Beverly A. Flowers, M.Div.
